// how it works

Three steps.
One owl.

deepowl turns raw documents into an interactive knowledge tutor that knows exactly what's in them.

01 / INGEST
📂
Point at your files

Drop any folder — PDFs, Markdown, plain text. deepowl parses, chunks intelligently, and stores vectors in a local ChromaDB instance on your machine.

02 / GRAPH
🕸️
Knowledge graph builds itself

NetworkX maps relationships between concepts across all your documents. Ideas that appear together get connected. You get a navigable web of knowledge.

03 / LEARN
🧠
Socratic dialogue begins

deepowl doesn't lecture — it asks you questions first, builds on your answers, and cites the exact source. Every response is grounded in your documents.

// features

Built different.

LOCAL & CLOUD LLM
Your choice of LLM

Run fully local with Ollama (qwen3, Llama 3, Mistral, DeepSeek) or connect to OpenAI, Anthropic, or Groq. Switch anytime — your data stays yours.

$ deepowl config set model.provider ollama
$ deepowl config set model.provider openai
$ deepowl config set model.provider anthropic
→ swap provider anytime
KNOWLEDGE GRAPH
Concepts, not just chunks

The LLM extracts concepts and relations from your docs, NetworkX builds the graph. deepowl understands how your ideas connect — not just which words match a query.

Knowledge Tracing ── BKT
             └── IRT ── DKT
Bayesian ──────────────┘
PEDAGOGY
Socratic, not encyclopedic

Inspired by Bloom's taxonomy and spaced repetition. deepowl guides you to answers through questions — it never just dumps information.

deepowl: What do you think causes...
 you: Maybe the gradient vanishes?
 deepowl: Correct — this is exactly why
          transformers replaced RNNs →
OPEN SOURCE
Apache 2.0 licensed. Fork it.

Every line of deepowl is on GitHub under the Apache 2.0 license. Extend it, embed it, contribute. Built for researchers, engineers, and lifelong learners who own their tools.
